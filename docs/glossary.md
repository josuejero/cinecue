# Core product glossary

- **Follow**: a user explicitly tracks a movie for local availability changes.
- **Nearby**: within the user’s saved ZIP or later distance rule.
- **Coming soon**: known upcoming title without current local playtimes.
- **Newly scheduled**: local showtimes appeared after previously being absent.
- **Now playing**: movie has active local showtimes.
- **Advance tickets**: future local showtimes are listed and tickets are available.
- **Stopped playing**: movie was previously local but no longer has active local showtimes.
